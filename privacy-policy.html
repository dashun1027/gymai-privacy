GymAI（以下稱「本 App」）由 GymAI 開發團隊（以下稱「我們」）開發與營運。 我們非常重視您的隱私，本政策說明本 App 會收集哪些資料、如何使用、如何保護， 以及您對自身資料所擁有的權利。當您安裝或使用本 App，即表示您已閱讀並同意本政策。

GymAI ("the App") is developed and operated by the GymAI Team ("we", "us"). We take your privacy seriously. By installing or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this policy.

1. 我們收集的資訊與權限宣告 / Information We Collect & Permissions

本 App 僅收集為提供健身紀錄與分析功能所必要的資料。以下為實際會使用到的權限：

The App only collects data necessary to provide fitness logging and analysis. The following permissions are actually used:

Apple Health（HealthKit）

經您明確授權後，本 App 可能讀取或寫入下列健康數據：步數、睡眠、心率、 體能訓練（Workout）與體重。這些資料僅用於在 App 內呈現您的訓練與恢復分析， 絕不外流、絕不販售、亦絕不用於任何廣告用途。 您可隨時於 iOS「設定 → 健康 → 資料存取與裝置」中撤回授權。

With your explicit consent, the App may read or write the following health data: steps, sleep, heart rate, workouts, and body weight. This data is used solely to display your training and recovery analysis inside the App, and is never shared, sold, or used for advertising. You may revoke access at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

身體組成數據 / Body Composition (InBody)

您手動輸入或透過掃描 InBody 報告所產生的體脂率、骨骼肌重、內臟脂肪等級、 部位別肌肉量等資料，將儲存於您的個人帳號中，僅用於趨勢追蹤與分析。

Data you enter manually or capture by scanning an InBody report — such as body fat percentage, skeletal muscle mass, visceral fat level, and segmental muscle analysis — is stored in your account and used only for trend tracking and analysis.

相機與相簿權限 / Camera & Photos

  • 相機：僅用於掃描有氧機台螢幕進行 OCR 文字辨識， 以及掃描 InBody 報告。影像於辨識後在裝置本地端處理， 我們不會在未經您操作的情況下保留原始照片。
  • 相簿（寫入）：僅用於將您在裝置本地端合成的訓練成果影片 / 圖卡 儲存到您的相簿，以便分享至 IG、FB 等社群。我們不會讀取或上傳您相簿中的 其他照片。
  • Camera: used only to scan cardio machine screens for OCR text recognition and to scan InBody reports. Images are processed on-device; we do not retain original photos without your action.
  • Photos (write): used only to save workout result videos/cards that are composed locally on your device, so you can share them to IG, FB, etc. We do not read or upload other photos from your library.

  • 產生個人化的訓練建議、恢復指數與成就系統。

  • Provide and maintain core features: training log, 1RM analysis, AI coach reports, InBody data center.

3. 資料儲存與安全 / Data Storage & Security

本 App 使用 Google Firebase（Cloud Firestore 與 Firebase Authentication） 進行帳號驗證與雲端資料同步。資料在傳輸與儲存過程中採用業界標準加密機制。 我們承諾絕不將您的個人資料或健康數據販售給任何第三方廣告商。

The App uses Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication) for account authentication and cloud sync. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard mechanisms. We will never sell your personal or health data to any third-party advertiser.

4. 第三方服務 / Third-Party Services

本 App 使用 Google Firebase 提供雲端基礎設施。相關資料處理受 Google 隱私權與安全政策規範。除提供服務所必要者外，我們不會與其他第三方分享您的個人資料。

The App relies on Google Firebase for cloud infrastructure; related processing is governed by Google's Privacy and Security policy. We do not share your personal data with other third parties except as necessary to provide the service.

您對自己的資料擁有完整掌控權。您可以隨時：

  • 於 App 內查看、修改您的個人資料與訓練紀錄。
  • 要求徹底刪除帳號與所有歷史健康紀錄：您可於 App 的 「個人檔案 → 設定 → 重置 / 刪除帳號」執行刪除，或來信至下方聯絡信箱提出申請。 一經執行，我們將從 Firebase 伺服器永久且不可復原地刪除您的所有資料。

You have full control over your data. At any time you may:

  • View and edit your profile and training records inside the App.
  • Request complete deletion of your account and all health history via Profile → Settings → Reset / Delete Account, or by emailing the address below. Once executed, all your data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from Firebase servers.
